# FairAC - Complete Feature List

*FairAC is a smart, automated electricity tracking and billing system designed specifically for university hostels and shared living spaces. It completely eliminates roommate disputes over AC bills by tracking exactly who used the AC and when.*

---

### 💸 Fair & Transparent Billing
- **Individual Billing:** No more splitting AC bills equally among roommates regardless of who actually used it. The system tracks exactly who turned the AC on and bills them only for their exact usage.
- **Real-Time Cost Tracking:** Students can see exactly how much money the AC is costing them in real-time, straight from their phone. No more surprise electricity bills at the end of the month!
- **Smart Sharing:** If multiple roommates are in the room, they can simply join the active AC session on their phones. The system will automatically and fairly split the cost of the electricity between everyone enjoying the cool air!
- **Built-In Digital Wallet:** Students have a digital wallet where they can add money. AC usage costs are automatically deducted from their wallet, completely eliminating the hassle of collecting cash or arguing over payments.

### 🧠 Smart & Automated Controls
- **Flexible Modes:** Students can choose to run the AC endlessly, set a time limit (e.g., run for 2 hours), or even set a budget (e.g., turn off automatically when ₹50 is spent).
- **Auto-Stop on Remote Use:** If a student gets too cold in the middle of the night, they can just turn off the AC with their normal TV-style remote control. The smart system physically detects that the AC stopped drawing power and automatically stops billing them—no need to wake up and open the app!
- **Mistake Forgiveness (Grace Period):** If a student accidentally turns on the AC but turns it off within 60 seconds, they aren't charged a penny. 
- **Anti-Spam Base Fee:** To prevent people from abusing the system by rapidly turning the AC on and off, any session longer than a minute incurs a tiny minimum base fee.

### 🛡️ Ironclad Security & Safety
- **Anti-Theft Protection:** If someone unplugs the internet router or tampers with the connection to try and get free electricity, the system's brain will realize it has lost contact with headquarters and will automatically shut off the AC after 5 minutes to prevent theft.
- **Automatic Offline Saving:** If the internet goes down naturally, the hardware has an internal memory that safely remembers the electricity usage. Once the internet comes back, it safely uploads the missing data so no usage is ever lost or unbilled.
- **Emergency Safety Button:** Every room is equipped with a physical red Emergency Stop button. If there is ever smoke, a spark, or an emergency, hitting the button instantly cuts all heavy power to the AC.
- **"Auto-Healing" Brain:** Heavy electrical appliances can create invisible "noise" that crashes standard electronics. FairAC's hardware is programmed to instantly detect if it has crashed and will silently reboot and fix itself in milliseconds without the student ever noticing.

### 🏢 Administrative Power
- **Administrator Dashboard:** University or hostel managers have a powerful, easy-to-use dashboard where they can see every room's AC usage, manage student wallets, and monitor overall electricity consumption across the entire building.
- **Live Room Displays:** Every room features a small digital screen on the wall showing the live electricity usage, the current session cost, and the status of the AC, so students always know what's happening without needing to look at their phones.
