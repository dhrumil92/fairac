A Story You Know Too Well.

🌅 You leave for college at 8 AM. Your roommate stays back and runs the AC on full blast all day.

02 / 11  ·  The Broken System

How Hostels Bill You Today

1

Start of Month

Owner walks around and notes the reading on each room's sub-meter.

2

End of Month

Owner walks around again and notes the new reading.

3

Calculate Bill

Difference in units × price per unit = Total Bill for the room.

4

Divide Equally — No Matter What

The total is split equally among all roommates, regardless of who used the AC, and for how long.

❌ No one tracks WHO used HOW MUCH. The honest student always loses.

03 / 11  ·  The Solution

"What if the AC only charged you for the exact time YOU were in the room?"

🔌

The Smart Box

A compact device installed near the AC switchboard. It measures electricity consumed, to the last watt, every single second.

📱

The Mobile App

Your digital wallet and remote control. Book the AC, watch your bill grow live, and split costs with roommates in real-time.

No more equal splits. No more arguments.
Just pay for exactly what you use.

06 / 11  ·  Safety & Trust

The Smart Box Never Forgets.

⚡

Survives Power Cuts

If the electricity goes out mid-session, the Smart Box saves your last reading — like a car's trip meter that doesn't reset. When power returns, it resumes from exactly where it left off.

🔒

Your Money is Safe

All wallet transactions are locked and protected. No roommate can cheat, modify, or reverse a payment. Every action is permanently recorded in your history.

🛡️

AC is Protected Too

The system enforces a 3-minute safety wait before any new session can start. This protects the AC's compressor from damage — just like a trusted technician would recommend.

🚨

Emergency Stop

A physical Emergency Stop button is built into the Smart Box. Anyone can instantly cut the power to the AC in an emergency, without needing the app at all.
