⚠ Safety Warning: The RED lines (220V AC) are dangerous high-voltage connections. These must only be wired inside a properly rated electrical enclosure (PVC/ABS DIN box) by a qualified electrician. The ESP32, relay, PZEM, LCD and LED circuits all operate on safe 3.3V / 5V DC. The 25A Contactor is the only component that switches the raw 220V AC to the air conditioner. The MCB upstream provides short-circuit and overload protection for the entire circuit.
