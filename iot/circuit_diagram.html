Breadboard Tip: The 30-pin ESP32 spans the full width of a standard 830-point breadboard, blocking the rows on both sides of the chip. By wiring all peripherals to LEFT-side GPIO pins only (GPIO 2, 4, 5, 15, 16, 17, 18, 19, 21, 22, 23), you can use the left columns (a–d) of the breadboard for all your jumper connections without crowding. The right-side pins (GPIO 12, 13, 14, 25, 26, 27, 32–39) remain unused and their rows stay free.

Original Prototype Note: This was the very first proof-of-concept using an ESP32-C3 SuperMini. The relay and PZEM are fully simulated in firmware. The only physical wiring is: USB → 5V, GPIO 8 → 220Ω → Blue LED → GND, and the built-in BOOT button on GPIO 9.

⚠ Safety: The 220V AC lines must be wired inside a properly rated electrical enclosure by a qualified electrician. All ESP32 and peripheral circuits operate on safe 3.3V / 5V DC only. The 25A Contactor is the only component that switches the raw 220V AC to the air conditioner.
